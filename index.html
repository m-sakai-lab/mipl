About the Lab

Humans sense multiple pieces of information using modalities known as the five senses. This type of information is called multimodal information. In the case of computers, modalities include video, audio, location information, behavioral information from acceleration sensors, and biometric information such as electrocardiograms, and artificial intelligence is used to grasp situations and make predictions. In this laboratory, we aim to use this type of multimodal information to discover and solve problems in our everyday lives.
