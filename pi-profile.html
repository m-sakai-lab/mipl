Principal Investigator

Motoki Sakai, Ph.D.

Associate Professor, Department of Computer Science, College of Engineering, Nihon University.

Dr. Sakai specializes in multimodal information processing, particularly focusing on communication research using data from various modalities such as voice, image, and sensor signals.

He leads the Multimodal Information Processing Laboratory with the aim of applying AI to real-world communication challenges.
